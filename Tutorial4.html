IMPORTANCE OF HEADINGS

1. HTML headings provide valuable information by highlighting important topics and the structure of the document, so optimize them carefully to improve user engagement.

2. Don't use headings to make your text look BIG or bold. Use them only for highlighting the heading of your document and to show the document structure.

3. Since search engines, such as Google, use headings to index the structure and content of the web pages so use them very wisely in your webpage.



Tip: Use the tag to mark the most important heading which is usually at the top of the page. An HTML document generally should have exactly one heading, followed by the lower-level headings
